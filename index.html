PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: July 16, 2025

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Stream Chat Hub operates entirely on your local device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or chat data to external servers. All data remains on your computer.

2. LOCAL DATA STORAGE

- Chat messages and events from connected platforms

- Application settings and preferences

- Channel configuration data

- Custom filter and notification settings

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Stream Chat Hub connects to third-party platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Twitch) using their public APIs. We only access publicly available chat data from your streams. We do not access private information or account credentials.

4. DATA SECURITY

Since all data is stored locally, you have complete control over your information. We recommend regular backups if you want to preserve your chat logs and settings.

5. CONTACT US

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@streamchathub.com
